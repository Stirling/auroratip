How It Works


1.
AuroraTip authenticates your identity when you Sign In via Twitter. A unique Auroracoin address is then created for you.

2.
Your account becomes activated when you deposit sufficient Auroracoins (1 AUR).

3.
Now you can send Auroracoins over twitter by tweeting at anyone (they do not need an account to initially receive Auroracoins). For example, "@friend, thanks bro, here's 0.01 AUR #auroratip". The 0.01 AUR in miner fees are automatically deducted from your account.

4.
The AuroraTip Bot, @auroratip, constantly listens for every tweet containing #auroratip or @auroratip. The Bot will then parse the tweet, looking for the recipient and send amount. (See Below)

5.
As soon as the funds are sent, both you and the recipient will receive a confirmation tweet.

6.
An account is automatically created for the recipient if they do not already have one. The recipient will then need to authenticate via twitter within 21 days to receive the Auroracoins, otherwise, the Auroracoins are automatically refunded to the sender.
